What is Music Theory?

Music theory operates at the confluence of the streams of philosophy, aesthetics, mathematics, physics, sociology, linguistics, psychology, logic, and theology, and it should explain music as a part of a complex, rich world, intimately united in itself.

    Notable music theorists:
  • Guido of Arezzo
  • Gioseffo Zarlino
  • Jean Rameau
  • Heinrich Schenker
  • Allen Forte
